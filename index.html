About Us

Bright Little Newton is dedicated to nurturing curious young minds aged 4 to 14. We focus on building strong foundations in Mathematics and English through engaging, hands-on, and interactive sessions. Our enrichment programs are designed to ignite a lifelong love for learning, helping every child reach their fullest potential!

Mission

Together we Learn, Grow, Care & Play. Empowering students with skills to succeed in life.

Mantra

Care at Core – Success starts here

Our Programs

  • Maths Enrichment – Logical reasoning, problem solving, mental maths, and advanced concepts for all levels.
  • English Enrichment – Reading, writing, vocabulary, grammar, and comprehension skills taught in a fun, supportive setting.
  • Interactive Workshops – Seasonal workshops, math games, spelling bees, story-writing and more!
  • Small Group Sessions – Personalized attention and tailored curriculum for each child.
